## Core User Journeys

### 1) Open Patient and Review Clinical Snapshot
- [ ] Staff can open patient from Live Feed.
- [ ] Patient demographics and key metrics are visible.
- [ ] Recent check-ins, EPDS, meals, and mood sections show data.

### 2) Review Full EPDS Questionnaire
- [ ] "View full questionnaire responses" link opens EPDS page.
- [ ] Staff sees selected answers, notes, and scores.
- [ ] Multiple audio clips (if present) play correctly.
- [ ] Back control returns to Patient Details.

### 3) Add and Review Clinical Notes
- [ ] Staff can save a clinical note.
- [ ] Existing notes remain visible as history.
- [ ] Notes appear in full track record view.

### 4) Refer Patient to Another Facility
- [ ] Staff can select destination and confirm referral.
- [ ] Referral history entry is created.
- [ ] Patient no longer appears in old facility queue after transfer.

### 5) Track Record and PDF
- [ ] Full track record page loads all major sections.
- [ ] PDF download works.
- [ ] PDF includes EPDS, meals, referrals, and clinical notes.
